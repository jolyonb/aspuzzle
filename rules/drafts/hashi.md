# Hashi Rules

Hashi (also known as Hashiwokakero or Bridges) is a puzzle played on a grid with numbered circles representing islands.

The objective is to connect all islands with bridges according to the following rules.

## Rules

1. Islands are represented by circles containing numbers from 1 to 8.
2. Bridges must connect islands in straight horizontal or vertical lines.
3. Bridges cannot cross other bridges or pass over islands.
4. At most two bridges may connect any pair of islands.
5. The number on each island indicates exactly how many bridges must connect to that island.
6. All islands must be connected into a single group (you must be able to travel from any island to any other island via bridges).

## Variations

* Hashi can be played on non-rectangular grids.
