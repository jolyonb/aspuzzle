# Thermometers Rules

Thermometers is a logic puzzle played on a rectangular grid containing thermometer shapes.

The objective is to fill thermometers with "mercury" to varying levels, guided by numeric clues outside the grid.

## Rules

1. Each thermometer may be filled with mercury starting from its bulb (the rounded end) toward its tip.
2. Mercury must be continuous within each thermometer - there cannot be gaps between filled cells.
3. A thermometer may be left completely empty, partially filled, or completely filled.
4. The numbers outside the grid indicate how many cells must be filled in each corresponding row and column.

## Variations

* Thermometers can appear in various orientations (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
* Some puzzles may have thermometers that span multiple rows or columns.
* Thermometers can be played on non-rectangular grids.
