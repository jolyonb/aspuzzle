# Shingoki Rules

Shingoki (also known as Semaphores) is a loop puzzle played on a grid with circles at various positions.

The objective is to draw a single continuous loop through the grid according to the following rules.

## Rules

1. Draw a single closed loop that passes through grid points, using horizontal and vertical line segments.
2. The loop cannot cross itself or branch.
3. The loop must pass through every circle (black and white) on the grid.
4. At white circles, the loop must pass straight through (no turn at the circle).
5. At black circles, the loop must turn 90 degrees.
6. Numbers on circles indicate the sum of the lengths of the two straight line segments extending from that circle.

## Variations

* Some variants include gray circles where the loop may either turn or go straight.
* Shingoki can be played on non-rectangular grids.
