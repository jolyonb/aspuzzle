# Masyu Rules

Masyu (also known as Pearl or Shiroshinju Kuroshinju) is a loop puzzle played on a rectangular grid with black and white circles.

The objective is to draw a single continuous loop through the grid according to the following rules.

## Rules

1. Draw a single closed loop that passes through cells using horizontal and vertical line segments.
2. The loop cannot cross itself or branch.
3. The loop must pass through every circle (black and white) on the grid.
4. At white circles, the loop must pass straight through (not turn), but must turn in at least one of the immediately adjacent cells (before or after the circle).
5. At black circles, the loop must turn 90 degrees, and must go straight through both cells immediately before and after the turn.

## Variations

* Masyu can be played on non-rectangular grids.
