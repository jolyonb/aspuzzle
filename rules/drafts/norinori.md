# Norinori Rules

Norinori is a shading puzzle played on a rectangular grid divided into regions.

The objective is to shade cells to form dominoes according to the following rules.

## Rules

1. Exactly two cells must be shaded in each region.
2. Every shaded cell must be part of a domino (a 1x2 or 2x1 group of exactly two orthogonally adjacent shaded cells).
3. Dominoes may span across region boundaries.
4. Dominoes cannot touch each other orthogonally (diagonal touching is allowed).

## Variations

* Norinori can be played on non-rectangular grids.
