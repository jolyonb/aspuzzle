# Yin-Yang Rules

Yin-Yang is a shading puzzle played on a rectangular grid where some cells may be pre-colored.

The objective is to color every cell either black or white according to the following rules.

## Rules

1. Every cell must be colored either black or white.
2. All black cells must be connected orthogonally in a single group.
3. All white cells must be connected orthogonally in a single group.
4. No 2x2 area may be entirely the same color.

## Variations

* Yin-Yang can be played on non-rectangular grids.
