# Heyawake Rules

Heyawake (Japanese for "divided rooms") is a shading puzzle played on a rectangular grid divided into regions.

The objective is to shade some cells black while leaving others white according to the following rules.

## Rules

1. Each cell must be either shaded (black) or unshaded (white).
2. Regions containing a number must have exactly that many shaded cells.
3. Shaded cells cannot be orthogonally adjacent to each other.
4. A horizontal or vertical line of consecutive white cells cannot span more than two regions.
5. All white cells must be connected orthogonally in a single group.

## Variations

* Some regions may have no number, meaning any number of cells may be shaded (subject to other rules).
* Heyawake can be played on non-rectangular grids.
