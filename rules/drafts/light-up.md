# Light Up Rules

Light Up (also known as Akari or Bijutsukan) is a puzzle played on a rectangular grid with black and white cells.

The objective is to place light bulbs in white cells so that every white cell is illuminated.

## Rules

1. Light bulbs can only be placed in white cells.
2. A light bulb illuminates all cells in its row and column, up to (but not including) any black cells that block the light.
3. Every white cell must be illuminated by at least one light bulb.
4. No light bulb may illuminate another light bulb (they cannot be in the same row or column without a black cell between them).
5. Some black cells contain numbers indicating exactly how many light bulbs must be orthogonally adjacent to that cell.
6. Black cells without numbers may have any number of adjacent light bulbs (including zero).

## Variations

* Light Up can be played on non-rectangular grids.
